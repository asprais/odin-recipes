Beef Empanadas

Description

This is a delicious Colombian beef empandas recipe.

Ingredients
  • Ground beef
  • Bell pepper
  • Potatoes
  • White and green onion
  • Cilantro
  • Goya Sazon packet
  • Garlic
  • Lime
  • Butter or cream
  • Maseca
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Cumin
Steps
  1. Cut up all vegetables.
  2. Mix maseca with hot water, Goya Sazon packet, salt, and a squeeze of lime. Let sit for 30 minutes.
  3. Begin boiling potatoes in a pot of water. When they are done, add butter or cream and mash.
  4. Place all green onions and part of white onion in a skillet.
  5. Wait for onions to soften, then add garlic.
  6. Add bell pepper and wait 20ish minutes for them to cook.
  7. Add ground beef.
  8. When most of grease from ground beef has simmered out, add the mixture to the mashed potatoes and mix.
  9. To form the empanadas, roll a ball of dough out of the maseca, smash it, and then place a small amount of the mixture in the middle of it.
  10. Fold one side of the flattened dough ball over itself, sealing the mixture inside.
  11. Fry the empandas until golden brown in a pan of oil.
